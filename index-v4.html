Hi! I'm Henrique Athayde, an independent artist and designer specializing in illustration, branding and character design.

For the past decade, I've been working with clients big and small in a range of industries, including entertainment, technology, publishing and board games.

In my work, I aim to combine precise, disciplined shapes and constructions with the natural imperfections and textures we only get from putting pencil and paint to paper. Unmistakably human-made, for other people to enjoy.
